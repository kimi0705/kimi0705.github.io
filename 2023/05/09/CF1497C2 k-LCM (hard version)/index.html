You are given a positive integernnn. Findkkkpositive integersa1,a2,…,aka_1, a_2, \ldots, a_ka1​,a2​,…,ak​, such that:

-a1+a2+…+ak=na_1 + a_2 + \ldots + a_k = na1​+a2​+…+ak​=n
