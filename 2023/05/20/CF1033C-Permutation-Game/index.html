  • the number in the new celljjjmust be strictly larger than the number in the old celliii(i.e.aj>aia_j > a_iaj​>ai​), and

提示

In the first sample, if Bob puts the token on the number (not position):

-111: Alice can move to any number. She can win by picking777, from which Bob has no move.
-222: Alice can move to333and555. Upon moving to555, Bob can win by moving to888. If she chooses333instead, she wins, as Bob has only a move to444, from which Alice can move to888.
-333: Alice can only move to444, after which Bob wins by moving to888.
-444,555, or666: Alice wins by moving to888.
-777,888: Alice has no move, and hence she loses immediately.
